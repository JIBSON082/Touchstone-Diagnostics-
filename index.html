How to Prepare for Your Test

Follow these instructions to ensure the most accurate diagnostic results.

Fasting Requirements

For Blood Sugar or Lipid Profiles, please fast for 8–12 hours. You may drink plain water only.

Pelvic Ultrasounds

Please drink at least 1 litre of water 1 hour before your scan. Do not empty your bladder.

Previous Records

Always bring your doctor's referral note and any previous scan results for comparison.
