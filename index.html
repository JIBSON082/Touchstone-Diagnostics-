About Touchstone Diagnostics

Located in Surulere, Touchstone Diagnostics is the gold standard for clinical precision. We believe accurate diagnosis is the touchstone of effective treatment. Our facility is equipped with advanced technology to ensure every result is delivered with speed and accuracy.

We specialize in cardiovascular health screening, cancer prevention, diabetes wellness, male & female fertility screening, imaging (X‑ray, ultrasound), laboratory medicine (hematology, microbiology, immunology), blood banking, and wellness packages.
