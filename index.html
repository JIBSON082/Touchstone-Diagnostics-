About Touchstone Diagnostics

At Touchstone Diagnostics, we provide an accurate foundation for your health journey through state-of-the-art laboratory and radiology services. Whether you require urgent cardiovascular screening, early cancer prevention, or specialized fertility diagnostics, our facility is equipped with modern medical technology to ensure clinical excellence.

Our team of experienced pathologists, radiologists, and laboratory scientists work round-the-clock to deliver accurate results with the compassion and care you deserve. We understand that behind every test result is a person seeking answers, and we're committed to providing those answers with precision and empathy.

Our Diagnostic Services

Clinical Laboratory

Haematology, clinical chemistry, hormone assays, and routine pathology with automated analyzers for accurate results.

Histopathology

Tissue biopsy, cancer screening, and surgical pathology for definitive diagnosis and treatment planning.

Cardiac Assessment

ECG, echocardiography, stress testing, and cardiac enzyme monitoring for comprehensive heart health evaluation.

Obstetric Scan

2D/3D/4D ultrasound, fetal doppler, anomaly scans, and pregnancy monitoring by experienced sonographers.

Fertility Screening

Hormone profiling, semen analysis, ovulation tracking, and comprehensive fertility assessment for couples.

Digital X-Ray

Low-dose digital radiography for chest, skeletal, and abdominal imaging with instant results.

CT Scan

128-slice helical CT for detailed cross-sectional imaging with minimal radiation exposure.

Health Check Packages

Executive health screening, pre-employment medicals, and customized wellness packages.
